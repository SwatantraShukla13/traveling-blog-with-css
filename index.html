Lucknow - 'City Of Nawabs'

Lucknow is a cultural hotspot in northen India for many centuries. Heritage, food, shopping whatever reason you travel for it has them all. In the comprehensive Lucknow travel blog, I have put together all you need you to know to travel to Lucknow including things to see Lucknow.

Things to see in Lucknow:-

  • Rumi Darwaza
  • Bada Imambara
  • Bhool Bhulaiya
  • Ghanta Ghar
  • Ambedkar Memorial Park
  • Janeshwar Park

Ayodhya - 'Birthplace of Lord Rama'

Ayodhya, Situated in the state of Uttar Pradesh, is an ancient city steeped in rich cultural and religious history. Renowned as the birthplace of Lord Rama, Ayodhya holds immense significance for millions of people worldwide.

Things to see in Ayodhya:-

  • The Scared Ram Janmbhoomi
  • Nagehswar Nath Temple
  • Ram Ki Paidi
  • Hanuman Garhi
  • Gulab Bari
  • Kanak Bhawan

Varanasi -'Oldest Holy City in the World'

Varanasi is one of the world's oldest cities, founded by Lord Shiva himself and huge scared site for pilgrims of the Hindu faith. It is also known as Banaras. It is an important place of life and death where people cremated and have thier ashes scattered into the holy River Ganges so they can to straight to heaven.

Things to see in Varanasi:-

  • Kashi Vishwanath Temple
  • Dashashwamedh Ghat
  • Assi Ghat
  • Tulsi Manas Temple
  • Banaras Hindu University
  • Alamgir Mosque
