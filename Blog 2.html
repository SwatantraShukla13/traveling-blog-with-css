Ayodhya - 'Birthplace of Lord Rama'


Ayodhya, Situated in the state of Uttar Pradesh, is an ancient city steeped in rich cultural and religious history. Renowned as the birthplace of Lord Rama, Ayodhya holds immense significance for millions of people worldwide.

Things to see in Ayodhya:-

  • The Scared Ram Janmbhoomi
  • Nagehswar Nath Temple
  • Ram Ki Paidi
  • Hanuman Garhi
  • Gulab Bari
  • Kanak Bhawan
